WHY SELL SOLAR NOW?
  • Less than 3% of homes in California currently have solar systems.
  • Electric Rates in California are the highest in the US and set to increase over 30% in 2017.
  • The US Government offers buyers a 30% tax credit on the purchase of a solar system (that's a 30% discount to all buyers).
  • A solar system is a non-market based investment with a guaranteed return on investment. As long as the sun comes up, the owner makes money.
  • The average homeowner will save $50,000-$100,000 over the 25 year lifetime of their solar system vs staying with their utility.
